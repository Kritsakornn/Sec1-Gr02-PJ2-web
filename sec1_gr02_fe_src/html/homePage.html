Frozen Food Retail is a dynamic business sector dedicated to the distribution and sale of food products preserved through freezing. This inventory typically spans several categories, including raw meats, poultry, seafood, vegetables, fruits, and increasingly popular processed "ready-to-eat" or "ready-to-cook" meals. By maintaining these items at sub-zero temperatures, the business effectively halts microbial growth, thereby preserving nutritional value, maintaining peak freshness, and significantly extending shelf life compared to fresh counterparts.
